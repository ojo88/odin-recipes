Description

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1/2 cup of peas
  • 1/2 cup of diced carrots
  • 1 1/2 pounds of potatoes
  • 3 tbsp of tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp of thyme
  • 1 tps of oregano
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/2 cup of chicken stock
  • 4 tbsp of butter
  • 1/2 pint of heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Place a large pot of water on the stove at medium-high heat for boiling the potatoes
  2. While the pot is boiling, skin the potatoes and dice into quarters.
  3. Boil potatoes for 20 minutes
  4. While the potatoes are boiling, place a pan on the stove at medium heat and brown the ground beef
