Shepherd's Pie

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1/2 cup of peas
  • 1/2 cup of diced carrots
  • 1 1/2 pounds of potatoes
  • 3 tbsp of tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp of thyme
  • 1 tps of oregano
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/2 cup of chicken stock
  • 4 tbsp of butter
  • 1/2 pint of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Place a large pot of water on the stove at medium-high heat for boiling the potatoes
  2. While the pot is boiling, skin the potatoes and dice into quarters.
  3. Boil potatoes for 20 minutes
  4. While the potatoes are boiling, place a pan on the stove at medium heat and brown the ground beef
  5. Season the beef with thyme and oregano.
  6. When the ground beef is close to full browning, add the carrots and peas. Mix well.
  7. After 2 minutes of cooking, add the tomato paste and stir in well.
  8. When you begin to see some oil separation between from the tomato paste and ground beef, stir in the Worcestershire sauce. Cook for 1 minute.
  9. Stir in the chicken stock and change the temperature to medium-low heat. Shoot for a low simmer.
  10. When the beef mixture thickens, remove from heat.
  11. Pre-heat the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
  12. Remove the potatoes from the stove and turn off the burner. Strain the water from the potatoes and place the potatoes back into the pot.
  13. Add in the 4 tbsp butter, heavy cream, parmesan cheese, and season the pot with salt in pepper.
  14. Use the potato masher and mash the potatoes into the desired consistency.
  15. Find a rectangular baking dish and pour in the beef mixture. Spread on the surface evenly.
  16. Spoon the mashed potatoes on top of the rectangular dish and spread carefully on over the beef mixture.
  17. Place the baking dish in the oven and cook for 25 minutes uncovered. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.
