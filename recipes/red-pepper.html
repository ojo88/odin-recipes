Red Pepper Sauce with Cavatappi

Description

This dish is one of the reasons I have to go on a low carb diet. There is not a name for this dish, but the recipe is from a popular Youtube cooking channel (Not Another Cooking Show). The Youtuber based the sauce from a dish he had in his teenage years while visiting family in Italy. I call the dish Red Pepper Sauce with Cavatappi and it's so delicious and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • A jar of calabrian peppers
  • 1 pint of heavy cream
  • Crushed red peppers
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil
  • Pecorino romano
  • 2 tbsp of tomato paste
  • 16 oz of cavatappi/cellentani
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Heat a large pan on the stove at medium heat.
  2. Roughly chop red bell peppers, onions and garlic and set aside.
  3. Add a tablespoon of olive oil to the pan.
  4. When the pan is hot, add the peppers, onions and garlic to the pan and saute.
  5. After 3 minutes or when the onions and peppers appear almost done, mix in two spoonfuls of calabrian peppers.
  6. Add 2 tablespoons of tomato paste and saute for a bit.
  7. After 3 or 4 minutes add a pint of heavy cream to the pan and stir sauce.
  8. Cook for 3 or 4 more minutes and then remove the pan from the stove.
  9. While hot, add the sauce to a blender and blend until it's a nice smooth consistency. The sauce is now ready and can be placed in a container to store for future use if desired.
  10. The stove should still be hot so place a pot of salted water on the stove to cook the cavatappi pasta. Cook the pasta for 1 or 2 minutes less than the recommended cooking time on box. We're going to finish cooking the cavatappi in the pasta sauce pan.
  11. While the cavatappi pasta is boiling in the pot, take this time to grate 1/3 of a cup of pecorino romano and set aside.
