Lasagna Soup

Description

This dish is a weekly favorite in my home. The thing that's great about lasagna soup is that it has all the great flavors from a traditional lasagna (a rich bolognese-esque meat sauce and delicious cheese) and it can be made in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb of Italian sausage
  • 1/2 lb of ground beef
  • 2 tbsp of tomato paste
  • 24 oz jar of Rao's Marinara
  • 4 garlic cloves chopped
  • 1 onion
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan reggiano
  • 1/4 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • Fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 8 oz of lasagna
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil

Steps

  1. Heat a large pan or pot on the stove at medium heat.
  2. Add 1 tbsp of olive oil to the hot pan and then mix in the chopped onions
  3. After the onions saute for a 2 minutes, add in the chopped garlic and mix for 1 minute.
  4. Add both the Italian sausage and ground beef and mix until browned. Use a potato masher to get the meat to a nice bite size and consistency
  5. Add the 2 tbsp of tomato paste to the meat and mix in evenly. Saute for 2 minutes.
  6. Add the jar of Rao's Marinara sauce to the pan and then stir.
  7. Season the pan with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. After a few minutes of sauteing, add in the heavy cream and stir until it is evenly distributed in the sauce. The sauce should change color from red to orange.
  9. Add the cup of chicken broth to the sauce and stir.
  10. Break the lasagna sheets into smaller pieces and drop them into the tomato sauce. Mix pasta into the sauce to ensure the sheets are covered in sauce.
  11. Cook until the pasta has softened to the desirable bite. You may need to add water to the sauce if it becomes to thick before the pasta is completely cooked
  12. When the pasta is cooked to the desired bite, remove the pan from the stove
  13. Adjust the stove temperature from medium heat to low heat.
  14. Add in Mozzarella cheese along with grated parmesan reggiano and the fresh basil into the lasagna soup and mix.
  15. Place the pan back onto the stove and heat a few minutes until the cheese has melted.
